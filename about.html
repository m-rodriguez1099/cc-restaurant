Q: What inspired you to open Celina's Cantina?

A: My family and I used to cook together a lot when I was growing up, a few years ago I realized I wanted to share our recipes with the rest of our community.

Q: Does your restaurant do any events?

A: Yes! We celebrate all holidays, you can ask a staff member for one of our monthly calendars to stay up to date on our events.

Q: What hours are your restaurant open?

A: Our restaurant is open from 8 AM to 9 PM, Sunday - Thursday. 8 AM to 11 PM, Friday - Saturday.

Q: You decided to open during quarantine, why did you choose to open during that time?

A: Well, I didn't want to necessarily open during quarantine but I felt it is what our community needed at the moment. I'm very thankful for the community we've built here.
